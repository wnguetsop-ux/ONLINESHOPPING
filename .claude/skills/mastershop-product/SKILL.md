# MasterShop Product

Use this skill when changing positioning, pricing, landing copy, flows from WhatsApp message to order, or plan structure.

## Core lens

- MasterShopPro organizes WhatsApp sales chaos.
- The app must help a merchant act faster, not learn a new complicated system.
- Keep the merchant's current selling behavior; improve the organization around it.

## Product priorities

1. Incoming WhatsApp messages
2. Clear client context
3. Draft or linked orders
4. Simple payment/status tracking
5. Controlled follow-up

## Product anti-patterns

- Generic ecommerce copy
- Feature bloat without clear merchant benefit
- Fancy automation that reduces trust
- Removing proof, context or guidance in the name of simplicity

## Pricing lens

- Prefer FCFA-first language
- Keep plans easy to compare
- Standard should usually be the hero offer
- Free should demonstrate value without replacing paid plans
